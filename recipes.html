Sweet Potato Chili

Ingredient Amount
beans (any) 2 cans
cubed sweet potato 4 cups
diced tomatoes 1 can
olive oil 1 tablespoon
large onion (white/red) 1 whole
garlic 3 cloves
jalapeno 1 whole
cumin 1 tbsp
smoked paprika 1 tbsp
ground chile powder 2 tbsp
oregano 1/2 tsp
vegetable broth 3 cups
Heat oil, cook onion and garlic until onion is translucent. Add seasoning and cook until fragrant (add liquid if needed). Add in rest of ingredients and cook for 30 minutes. Add lime, avacado and cheese after.

Broccoli and Potato Soup

Add potatoes, onions, broth, salt and pepper into crock pot. Turn on high for 4 hours. After 3 hours, blend/mash potatoes after they are soft. Add milk + cream, broccoli and cook on high for remaining 1 hour. Add vegan cheese after.

Ingredient Amount
white peeled potatos 7-9
white/red onion 1 whole
vegetable broth 2 cups
broccoli 2-3 cups
almond milk 2 cups
1/2 sour cream (otional)
